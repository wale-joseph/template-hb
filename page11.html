FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the next cohort start?

The next cohort starts every third week of the month except otherwise stated

Do I need to know any programming language before joining the fellowship?

No. The program is designed in such a way to provide you with adequate knowledge of at least 3 programming languages (Python, R, BASH). They are the most demanded combination for most bioinformatics roles.

What are the requirements for joining the fellowship?

The requirement for the specialization programs is to be a graduate (BS, BSc) of a life or natural science discipline. However, the interest tracks are designed for undergraduates, so no formal degree is required.

What is the difference between the fellowship specializations and interest packs?

Specialization programs are designed for persons with at least a formal degree in a life science, while the interest packs are designed for starters (usually undergraduates) with an unclear career direction. In both cases, we provide a support framework for academic/industry career mentorship. 

I already know about bioinformatics, I just want to understand research and job process...

Yes, you can still apply. Once you pass our initial assessment, we accelerate you to the Gym phase where you replicate previous research and perform your own innovative research ideas. You also take at least two mock job interviews for a biotech company and a research (MSc/PhD/PostDoc) position.

Would you link me with employers for a direct employment?

No. But we hope to in the future

What is the duration of the fellowship?

The fellowship is a 2 month program.

Do you provide scholarships for the program?

No. The program is already subsidized.

Can I pay in installments for the program?

Yes. For the specialization, you can decide to pay in a maximum of two installments.
