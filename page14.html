Specializations

Specializations

Specializations are specific applications of bioinformatics that are taught during the HackBio Fellowship. It is designed to gently immerse you into the deep basics of bioinformatics and rapidly transforms your coding, analytics and omics skill-sets. 
To enroll in the fellowship, we require that you have at least a formal academic degree (BS/BSc +) in a life science discipline.
By the end, you would be skilled, experienced and confident to take academic and industry roles. To know how we designed the content, read our 2021-Q1 industry report.

Interest Packs

Interest Packs

Interest Packs are specific units of core and applied bioinformatics that are designed for undergraduates with little or no career specialization. It is designed to gently immerse you into the deep basics of interesting unit (standalone applicaations of bioinformatics) and rapidly transforms one of your coding, analytics and omics skill-sets. 
To enroll for the interest pack, we require that you are motivated. No formal degree is required.
By the end, you would be skilled, experienced and confident to take academic and industry roles. To know how we designed the content, read our 2021-Q1 industry report.
